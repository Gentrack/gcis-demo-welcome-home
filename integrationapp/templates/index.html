Welcome Home

Positive customer experience in onboarding reflects on your brand and can drive high value referral from a customer's closest network. So when a new customer signs up it is important that you put your best foot forward to welcome them. In this demo we show how you can use MailChimp, a best-in-class marketing automation tool, to drive your welcome pack experience. This opens up potential for sending people on different welcome journeys depending on how they came to be a customer, A/B testing options to drive further engagement, and all of the great features you expect from marketing automation.

For more information on this demo see the source repository.
